Johnny Cakes

Johnny Cakes (Journey Cakes)

Split, buttered and eaten with a slice of Edam cheese or ham, this quick bread is traditional for breakfast or mid-morning snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 C flour

  • 4 Tbs shortening

  • 3 tsp Baking Powder

  • 1 tsp Salt

  • 1 pk coconut milk

Method

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Mix flour, shortening, baking powder and salt. Slowly add in enough water / coconut milk to make soft dough. Knead gently.
  3. Form into balls and with greased palms press the dough balls into the desired size and thickness.
  4. Pierce the top of the cakes several times with the tines of a fork to allow steam to escape.
  5. Bake until lightly brown approximately 30-45 minutes.
